Recipes

  • Spaghetti and Meatballs

    A classic Italian dish of spaghetti and juicy meatballs in a rich tomato sauce.

    Ingredients:

    • 1 pound spaghetti
    • 1 pound ground beef
    • 1 egg
    • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
    • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes

    Instructions:

    1. Cook the spaghetti according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
    2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, egg, breadcrumbs, parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, dried basil, and dried oregano.
    3. Shape the mixture into 1 1/2 inch meatballs.
    4. In a large saucepan, brown the meatballs in a small amount of oil over medium heat.
    5. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
    6. Serve the meatballs and sauce over the cooked spaghetti.
