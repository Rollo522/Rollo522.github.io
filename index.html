Recipe 3

Ingredients

    li>1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Grease a 9-inch round cake pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Stir in vanilla extract.
  6. Gradually add in flour mixture, alternating with milk, until just combined.
  7. Pour batter into prepared cake pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
