Dissertation Highlights

How AI mimics brand voices in phishing attacks.

Endorsed for Publication

My research evaluated the convincing nature of AI-generated phishing emails. By conducting style-matching experiments and user studies, I analysed how effectively large language models can replicate a brand's specific tone to deceive even cautious users.

The Objective

Helping organisations identify linguistic gaps in AI-generated scams to improve training and detection.

The Methodology

Benchmarking AI against legitimate brand communication using style-matching metrics.
