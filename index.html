02. Research Spotlight

Dissertation Endorsed for Publication

Evaluating Brand Imitation and Linguistic Realism in AI-Generated Phishing Emails

Executive Summary

This paper investigated the impact that branding has on phishing success and how AI might be utilised to aid attackers in carrying out more deceptive email attacks. The objective is to provide valuable information to help improve phishing detection methods in a world increasingly placing trust and reliance in AI. Object evaluation experiments are carried out to evaluate how well AI carries out linguistical style matching and emotional tone accuracy in replicating a brand's voice within phishing emails. Additionally, a user study is also deployed to analyse how users perceive different types of real and AI-generated emails of the phishing and legitimate variety, with the aim of identifying which aspects influenced misclassification the most and whether users could detect AI-generated content.
Impact Analysis

Analysed success rates of brand-mimicking AI against human detection capabilities in controlled experimental environments.
