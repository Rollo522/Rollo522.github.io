      • Spaghetti Carbonara

        Ingredients: spaghetti, eggs, pancetta or bacon, black pepper, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, salt

        Instructions:

        1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
        2. In a separate pan, cook the pancetta or bacon until crisp. Remove from heat and set aside.
        3. Beat the eggs in a bowl and add in grated Pecorino Romano cheese and black pepper.
        4. When the spaghetti is cooked, drain it and add it to the pan with the pancetta or bacon.
        5. Add the egg and cheese mixture to the pan, and toss everything together until the eggs are cooked and the cheese has melted.
        6. Serve immediately, with additional grated Pecorino Romano cheese and black pepper on top.

      • Chocolate Chip Cookies

        Ingredients: all-purpose flour, baking soda, salt, unsalted butter, granulated sugar, light brown sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, semisweet chocolate chips

        Instructions:

        1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
        2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
        3. In a large bowl, cream the butter, granulated sugar, and light brown sugar together until light and fluffy.
        4. Add in the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
        5. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, and mix until just combined.
        6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
        7. Use a cookie scoop or tablespoon to drop dough balls onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
        8. Bake for 8-10 minutes, until the edges are lightly golden brown. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
